The promotion test — one question per gate

SA → Senior"Are they making calls on their own?"
Senior → Advisory"Do other SAs bring them their hardest problems?"
Advisory → Principal"Would the region's capability drop if they left?"

The promotion timing rule

A promotion case needs to show the upper end of the current level and evidence of consistently operating at the next level for at least 2 quarters. Doing it once isn't ready. Doing it consistently — without being asked — is.

The one thing most frameworks miss

Promotion is not about doing more things. It is about owning things at a higher quality bar, with less direction, for a wider audience. A Senior SA who makes the team better as a side effect of their work is already Advisory.
